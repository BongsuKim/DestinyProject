Percent of Weapons Used for Each Combat Rating

A look at the frequency of use of the top 20 weapons in each combat rating group

View project on GitHub

This graph should be read as y percent of players with a combat rating of x use color weapon. It is imporant to note that these percentages do not add up to 100. In fact, stacking these bins will actually show many of them exceed 100. That is because players can be counted twice if they use multiple weapons in a game. It is still correct to say that y percent of players with a combat rating of x use color weapon. It is incorrect to say that y percent of players with a combat rating of x use only color weapon.
