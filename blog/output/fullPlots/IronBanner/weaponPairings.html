Frequency of Primary/Secondary Weapon Pairings

A look at how primary and secondary weapons are paired together

View project on GitHub

This graph shows how frenquently weapons are paired together over the entire set of data. The x-axis is primary weapons, and the colors represent different secondary weapons. When hovering over a bar, you should read the frequency as: y percent of players use x and color combo. For example, 16 percent of players use a Hand Cannon and Shotgun combo. None is for players who go through an entire game without using a primary weapon, but do have registered secondary weapon kills.
